Five books, five ideas
  • Passion for discovery
  • Not harmed
  • Manners matter
  • Life instead of afterlife
  • Sharing reality

Age of Wonder by Richard Holmes

In the 18OO:s both poets and scientists shared the same passion for discovery. Science and the Arts were once friends.

Meditations by Marcus Aurelius

Might as well accept that you cannot change the past or other people's opinions. "Choose not to be harmed — and you won't feel harmed."

The Path by Michael Puett and Christine Gross-Loh

Good manners make the next person behave better. Say "Please" and "Thank you".

The Swerve by Stephen Greenblatt

Roman poet Lucretius imagined atoms long before they were discovered. The meaning of life does not depend on the afterlife.

Letters From Prison by Milan Šimečka

I still hold on to kindness and integrity under brutal oppression. I respect your reality. And now come visit mine.
